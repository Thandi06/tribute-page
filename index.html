Dr. Zethu Princess Sithole

The young woman who became a doctor at the age of 30 years

  • In 2014 - Dr. Sithole earned her Education degree from Durban University of Technology, Ndumiso Campus.
  • In 2015 - She obtained her Honours degree in Education from the University of South Africa.
  • In 2018 - She earned her Master's degree in Education from the University of Mysore in India.
  • In 2023 - She graduated with her Doctorate from the University of Zululand.
